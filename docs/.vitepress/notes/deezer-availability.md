#### Deezer Free Availability

Deezer Free (with ads) is available in: United States, Brazil, Germany, France, Canada, Great Britain, UAE, Belgium, Ireland, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, and Ukraine. In the United States, Deezer Free is only available on mobile, desktop is limited to 30-second previews.
